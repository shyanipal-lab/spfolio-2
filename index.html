What's on my mind

Craft

Why do so many apps feel identical in 2025? We have more tooling, more AI, more references — and yet fewer products feel genuinely alive. We've optimised for components and forgotten about choreography.

AI × Design

AI won't replace designers who understand why something feels right. It will replace those who can only explain what something looks like. The gap between taste and generation is the new design skill.

Mobile

The best mobile interactions are the ones you never consciously notice. A perfectly timed haptic, a spring curve that anticipates momentum — this is the craft layer most teams skip. It's where I live.

Systems

Design systems aren't documentation — they're decisions made in advance. The best ones encode the team's philosophy so deeply that any new screen feels inevitable.
