Design Psychology

Principles behind the decisions

Hick's Law
Fewer choices. Faster decisions.
Show only what needs action now. One CTA per screen. No overload.
Zeigarnik Effect
Incomplete tasks demand attention.
Unpaid invoices surface first. We channel that fixation into action, not stress.
The Solution

Four features. That's it.

Every feature maps directly to a pain Priya named herself.

01
Track payments at a glance
Dashboard like a banking app — received, pending, overdue.
02
Send payment links via WhatsApp
One tap. Money lands in Priya's bank. No awkward texts.
03
Auto-generate monthly invoices
Generated from subscriber data. Review and send in seconds.
04
Manage subscribers & plans
Add subscribers, set prices, track who's active.

Lessons

03
Constraints ship products
